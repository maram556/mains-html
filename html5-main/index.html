SKYDIVING IS MY PASSION

skydiving, Sport of jumping from an airplane at a moderate altitude (e.g., 6,000 ft [1,800 m]) and executing various body maneuvers before pulling the rip cord of a parachute. Competitive events include jumping for style, landing with accuracy, and performing in teams (e.g., making free-fall formations). The sport parachute is designed to be more maneuverable than the safety parachute.

SKYDIVING ARCH TIPS HOW TO DO IT Learning to arch is a simple body position to master, but it doesn't come completely naturally to everyone. If you've ever done the limbo before, this is essentially the skill required-pelvis forward while arching your back with chin high. To feel the sensation of the arch, lay on the ground and extend your arms out while keeping them off the ground. Do the same thing with your legs-legs extended with a slight knee bend and keeping them off the floor.
